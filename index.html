About Me

I've spent my career at the intersection of entrepreneurship, technology, and practical problem-solving for businesses.

Early on, I lived in South Africa, where I worked with small financial nonprofits to help them adopt technology and improve how they operated. That experience gave me a deep appreciation for resource constraints and the importance of simple, effective tools—lessons that continue to shape how I approach problem-solving today.

In 2010, I became part of the founding team behind Startup Weekend, working with aspiring entrepreneurs to turn ideas into real companies in just a few days. That experience inspired me to start building companies myself and to fully experience what it means to be a founder.

I went on to co-found an AI-powered virtual assistant platform for banks, which was ultimately acquired by Envestnet | Yodlee. During that journey, I developed a firsthand understanding of the challenges of running a business—from product development to scaling—and how critical access to top-tier developer talent is in bringing ambitious ideas to life.

Since then, I've worked closely with boutique private equity and financial services firms to modernize their operations. My work has focused on improving financial reporting, strengthening accounting systems, and implementing more efficient, tech-enabled processes that support better decision-making.

What excites me most today is the rapid evolution of AI. The tools now available are incredibly powerful—and for small business owners, they unlock the ability to operate with a level of leverage that simply wasn't possible before.

I'm a naturally curious person who enjoys understanding how things work and finding better ways to do them. If you're running a business and looking to simplify operations, save time, or unlock new capabilities through AI, I'd love to help.
